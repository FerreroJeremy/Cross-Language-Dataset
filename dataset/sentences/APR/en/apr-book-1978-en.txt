this book is a little masterpiece that could also be the scenario of a good thriller.
the plot that unfolds in france in the pyrenees, is conducted with brio from first to last page, the characters are quite endearing and it fluctuates constantly between reality and the paranormal.
i defy anyone who would not shudder at the turn of the purposes of chapters and do not rush to the next to hear the end of this story very mysterious disappearance ...
