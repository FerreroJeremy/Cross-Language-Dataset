Commission Regulation (EC) No%nbsp%164/2006 of 30 January 2006 altering the export refunds on white sugar and raw sugar exported in the natural state fixed by Regulation (EC) No%nbsp%93/2006 Commission Regulation (EC) No 164/2006 of 30 January 2006 altering the export refunds on white sugar and raw sugar exported in the natural state fixed by Regulation (EC) No 93/2006 THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, Having regard to the Treaty establishing the European Community, Having regard to Council Regulation (EC) No 1260/2001 of 19 June 2001 on the common organisation of the markets in the sugar sector [1], and in particular the third subparagraph of Article 27(5) thereof, Whereas: (1) The export refunds on white sugar and raw sugar exported in the natural state were fixed by Commission Regulation (EC) No 93/2006 [2].
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on 31 January 2006.
Done at Brussels, 30 January 2006.
