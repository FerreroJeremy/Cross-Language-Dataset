Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4362 — voestalpine/K + N/Euroshipping) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4362 — voestalpine/K + N/Euroshipping) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 207/05) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 23 August 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertakings voestalpine AG (%quot%voestalpine%quot%, Austria) and Kühne + Nagel International AG (%quot%K + N%quot%, Switzerland) acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of the undertaking Kühne + Nagel Euroshipping GmbH (%quot%Euroshipping%quot%, Germany) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
