Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4413 — Apollo Group/GE Advanced Materials) Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4413 — Apollo Group/GE Advanced Materials) (2006/C 267/04) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 24 October 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the Apollo Group (%quot%Apollo%quot%, United States), which controls inter alia the chemical company Hexion Specialty Chemicals, Inc. (%quot%Hexion%quot%), acquires within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation control of the whole of GE Advanced Materials (%quot%GEAM%quot%, United States) and the joint ventures GE Bayer Silicones (%quot%GEBS%quot%) and GE Toshiba Silicones (%quot%GETOS%quot%) by way of purchase of shares. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Apollo: portfolio investments, via Hexion: manufacture and sale of various thermoplastic resins and related products; - for GEAM: manufacture and sale of silicones and related products; fused quartz and ceramic materials; - for GEBS: manufacture and sale of silicones and related products mainly in Europe; - for GETOS: manufacture and sale of silicones and related products in Asia. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4413 — Apollo Group/GE Advanced Materials, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1. --------------------------------------------------
