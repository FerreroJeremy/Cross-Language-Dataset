Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4311 — Philips/BenQ/PBDS) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4311 — Philips/BenQ/PBDS) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 161/02) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 3 July 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which Koninklijke Philips Electronics N.V. (%quot%Philips%quot%, Netherlands) and BenQ Corporation (%quot%BenQ%quot%, Taiwan), acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of Philips %amp% BenQ Digital Storage Corporation (%quot%PBDS%quot%, Taiwan) by way of transfer of assets. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for Philips: design, manufacture and sale of broad range of electronic products, including drives and media for CD and DVD, - for BenQ: communications and multimedia devices, - for PBDS: development, manufacture and sale of optical data storage drives (ODD) for personal computers. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M. 4311 — Philips/BenQ/PBDS to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
