Article 2 Prohibitions Fishing for the stock referred to in the Annex to this Regulation by vessels flying the flag of or registered in the Member State referred to therein shall be prohibited from the date set out in that Annex.
Article 3 Entry into force This Regulation shall enter into force on the day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
