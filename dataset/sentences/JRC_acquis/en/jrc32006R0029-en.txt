Commission Regulation (EC) No%nbsp%29/2006 of 10 January 2006 amending Regulation (EC) No%nbsp%2037/2000 of the European Parliament and of the Council with regard to customs codes for bromochloromethane Commission Regulation (EC) No 29/2006 of 10 January 2006 amending Regulation (EC) No 2037/2000 of the European Parliament and of the Council with regard to customs codes for bromochloromethane THE COMMISSION OF THE EUROPEAN COMMUNITIES, Having regard to the Treaty establishing the European Community, Having regard to Regulation (EC) No 2037/2000 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 29 June 2000 on substances that deplete the ozone layer [1] and in particular Article 6(5) thereof; Whereas: (1) The ozone-depleting substance bromochloromethane (BCM) is listed as a controlled substance in Group IX of Annex I to Regulation (EC) No 2037/2000. (2) Following the establishment of customs codes for BCM and mixtures containing BCM, those codes should be added to the table in Annex IV to Regulation (EC) No 2037/2000.
Article 2 This Regulation shall enter into force on the 20th day following its publication in the Official Journal of the European Union.
Done at Brussels, 10 January 2006.
For the Commission Stavros Dimas Member of the Commission [1] OJ L 244, 29.9.2000, p. 1.
