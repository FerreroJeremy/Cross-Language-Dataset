Communication from the Council 
(2004/C 277/01) 
On 5 May 2004 the Kingdom of Belgium deposited the instruments of ratification for the following: 
- the First Protocol on the interpretation by the Court of Justice of the European Communities of the Convention on the law applicable to contractual obligations, opened for signature in Rome on 19 June 1980 [1]; 
- the Second Protocol conferring on the Court of Justice of the European Communities certain powers to interpret the Convention on the law applicable to contractual obligations, opened for signature in Rome on 19 June 1980 [2]; 
- the Convention on the accession of the Republic of Austria, the Republic of Finland and the Kingdom of Sweden to the Convention on the law applicable to contractual obligations, opened for signature in Rome on 19 June 1980, and to the First and Second Protocols on its interpretation by the Court of Justice, signed in Brussels on 29 November 1996 [3]. 
Following the deposit of these instruments: 
- the First Protocol enters into force on 1 August 2004, in accordance with Article 6 thereof, for Member States having already ratified it; 
- the Second Protocol enters into force for all participating Member States on 1 August 2004; 
- the Convention referred to in the third indent enters into force for the Kingdom of Belgium, in accordance with Article 6 thereof, on 1 August 2004.
