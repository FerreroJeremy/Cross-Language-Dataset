Lifting by France of the public service obligations for scheduled air services between Strasbourg and Munich 
(2006/C 276/06) 
(Text with EEA relevance) 
France has decided to lift the public service obligations imposed on scheduled air services between Strasbourg and Munich as published in Official Journal of the European Communities C 85 of 9 April 2002 and revised in the Official Journal of the European Communities C 257 of 25 October 2003.
