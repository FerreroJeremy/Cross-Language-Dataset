Extension of the Community action programme in the field of civil protection * 
European Parliament legislative resolution on the proposal for a Council decision amending Decision 1999/847/EC as regards the extension of the Community action programme in the field of civil protection (COM(2004)0512 â&#x20AC;&#x201D; C6-0111/2004 â&#x20AC;&#x201D; 2004/0162(CNS)) 
(Consultation procedure) 
The European Parliament, 
- having regard to the Commission proposal to the Council (COM(2004)0512) [1], 
- having regard to Article 308 of the EC Treaty, pursuant to which the Council consulted Parliament (C6-0111/2004), 
- having regard to Rule 51 of its Rules of Procedure, 
- having regard to the report of the Committee on the Environment, Public Health and Food Safety (A6-0050/2004), 
1. Approves the Commission proposal; 
2. Calls on the Council to notify Parliament if it intends to depart from the text approved by Parliament; 
3. Asks the Council to consult Parliament again if it intends to amend the Commission proposal substantially; 
4. Asks the Commission to present a report to the European Parliament and the Council by 30 June 2006, with an evaluation of the extended Community action programme in the field of civil protection; 
5. Instructs its President to forward its position to the Council and the Commission. 
[1] Not yet published in OJ.
