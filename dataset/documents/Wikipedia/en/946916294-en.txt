Mil Helicopters (Миль) is the short name of the Soviet Russian helicopter producer Mil Moscow Helicopter Plant () (design bureau prefix Mi), named after the constructor Mikhail Mil. Mil participates in the Euromil joint venture with Eurocopter.Mil merged with Kamov and Rostvertol to form Oboronprom Corp in 2006.
