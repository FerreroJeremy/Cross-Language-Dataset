Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4352 — Goldman Sachs/MatlinPatterson/SecurLog) — Candidate case for simplified procedure Text with EEA relevance Prior notification of a concentration (Case COMP/M.4352 — Goldman Sachs/MatlinPatterson/SecurLog) Candidate case for simplified procedure (2006/C 222/07) (Text with EEA relevance) 1.
On 7 September 2006, the Commission received a notification of a proposed concentration pursuant to Article 4 of Council Regulation (EC) No 139/2004 [1] by which the undertakings The Goldman Sachs Group Inc. (%quot%Goldman Sachs%quot%, USA) and MatlinPatterson LLC (%quot%MatlinPatterson%quot%, USA) acquire within the meaning of Article 3(1)(b) of the Council Regulation joint control of the undertaking SecurLog GmbH (%quot%SecurLog%quot%, Germany) by way of purchase of shares and other securities. 2.
The business activities of the undertakings concerned are: - for undertaking Goldman Sachs: global investment banking and securities; - for undertaking MatlinPatterson: investment fund specialized in companies in financial difficulties and directing their reorganization; - for undertaking SecurLog: provider of cash services and secure courier services. 3.
Observations can be sent to the Commission by fax (fax No (32-2) 296 43 01 or 296 72 44) or by post, under reference number COMP/M.4352 — Goldman Sachs/MatlinPatterson/SecurLog, to the following address: European Commission Directorate-General for Competition Merger Registry J-70 B-1049 Bruxelles/Brussel [1] OJ L 24, 29.1.2004, p. 1.
