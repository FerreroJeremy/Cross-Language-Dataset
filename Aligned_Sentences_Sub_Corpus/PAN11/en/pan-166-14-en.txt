After the fleet arrives at the river Paraguay, to shorten the journey go by a narrow arm the same river, which they call Paraguay-mini arm, and makes the big island of Paraguay ten miles long, and in my opinion, they called the old Island Orejones Argentina puts because the Xarayes below.
Sail, after emerging from the arm, by the Paraguay river, until they reached a narrow arm of the Porrudos river, and to this arm narrow channel called Chan.
In leaving it, sail the river of Porrudos above, to enter the Cuiaba river coming from north to south.
Finally navigate the Cuiaba river above to the port city of the same name.
