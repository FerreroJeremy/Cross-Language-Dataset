this book takes alain finkielkraut writing some of the debates on his show replica france culture. it is often a pleasant book to read (thanks to the dialogue form) on contemporary debates: immigration and integration, the republic today, there was a french fascism?'s legacy de gaulle / mitterrand, france in the blackness, the jews / catholics in france ... i appreciated the great quality of the speakers even if the interests of debate is sometimes uneven. one of the most successful is the one between rené rémond and zeev sternhell on french fascism. in short, a book that will delight fans of magazines (like the notebook) or arouse the interest of all those interested in the history of france and the evolution of french society.
